SERVICES

Party Divas provides staff and planning for any occasion within your home. Our job is to make you, the host, look good while eliminating stress before, during, and after the event.

Our specialty:

  • Party Set up
  • Greet guests
  • Take coats
  • Warm and Set Food
  • Serve Drinks
  • Direct Flow of the Event
  • Clear Plates and Glassware
  • Clean the Kitchen and Party Space

Party Divas are:

  • Extremely organized
  • Friendly
  • Professional
  • Helpful
  • Hard working
  • Experienced with backgrounds in Planning, Catering, Events, and Serving
  • Insured
We Help With:
  • Themed parties

  • Holiday dinners
  • Fundraisers
  • Barbeques
  • Picnics
  • Engagement Parties
  • Bridal and Baby Showers
